Art & Soul Tattoos, Los Angeles,was established in 1996. As everyone knows, for tattoos, Los Angeles has some of the best artists in the world. Our friendly tattoo shop has been a cornerstone for L.A. tattoos & culture for over seventeen years. Our alumni boasts a plethora of uber-talented international tattoo names from Australia, Europe, Japan, and all over the good ole USA who have been making the world a more beautiful place with their ink.
